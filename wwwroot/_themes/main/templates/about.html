Hudson Woods serves as a powerful model to showcase a dialogue between the built and natural environment. These dwellings are more than just places, they are experiences shared between family, friends, and neighbors. Through connected design, nuanced attention to detail, and a commitment to sustainability, we were able to work with nature to add value and meaning to these unique residences.

“Our mission is to create authentic, meaningful experiences at every turn.”

Drew Lang, Lang Architecture

It took a village of makers, builders, and designers to make Hudson Woods what it is today. As a result, every home has been sold and the community is thriving. If you’d like to learn more about our approach to architecture and development, please visit our site.
